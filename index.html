Who we are

GAPInsurTech LLC (d/b/a ProteqTech) is a licensed managing general agency built to bring modern technology to specialty auto and ancillary insurance products.

Through our platform, ProteqTech, independent agents access streamlined quoting, binding, and commission tools across GAP, VSC, A&H, and Flood lines — backed by A-rated carrier partners.

GAPInsurTech holds the carrier appointments and regulatory standing behind the platform; ProteqTech is the technology brand agents use every day.

Leadership
Jason Walters
Founder & CEO

Jason Walters is the Founder and CEO of GAPInsurTech LLC, d/b/a ProteqTech. He brings 20 years of sales experience to the company, and founded ProteqTech to bring the same technology-first discipline to the insurance MGA space — building a platform that gives independent agents faster quoting, cleaner commission tracking, and easier access to specialty carriers across GAP, VSC, A&H, and Flood lines. He leads product, technology, and overall company strategy. Jason's focus is building the infrastructure that lets independent agents compete like national carriers — without the overhead.

Chris Walters
Co-Founder, Carrier Relations & Licensed Producer

Chris Walters is Co-Founder of ProteqTech and serves as the company's Licensed Producer of Record. He is also the owner of Integrity Insurance Group, an independent insurance agency serving West Virginia, bringing firsthand agency-owner experience to ProteqTech's carrier relationships and distribution strategy. Chris leads carrier relationships and distribution strategy for ProteqTech — ensuring the platform's product lineup reflects what agents actually want to sell and what carriers are willing to stand behind.
